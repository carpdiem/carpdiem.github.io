This is a collection of some of the best books I've ever encountered on various subjects, all linked to the idea of "how to be an effective human".

### Athletic Training and Physical Health

- [Starting Strength, by Mark Rippetoe](https://www.amazon.com/Starting-Strength-Basic-Barbell-Training/dp/0982522738/)

- [Tactical Barbell, by K. Black](https://www.amazon.com/Tactical-Barbell-Definitive-Strength-Operational-ebook/dp/B01G195QU2/)

- [Ultimate Back Fitness and Performance, by Stuart McGill](https://www.amazon.com/Ultimate-Fitness-Performance-Sixth-Stuart-McGill/dp/0973501839/)

### Social and Emotional Interaction

- [What Every Body Is Saying, by Joe Navarro](https://www.amazon.com/What-Every-Body-Saying-Speed-Reading/dp/0061438294/)

- [Never Split the Difference, by Chris Voss](https://www.amazon.com/Never-Split-Difference-Negotiating-Depended/dp/0062407805/)

- [Impro: Improvisation and the Theatre, by Keith Johnstone](https://www.amazon.com/Impro-Improvisation-Theatre-Keith-Johnstone/dp/0878301178/)

### Clear Communication, both Verbal and Written

- [Language in Thought and Action, by S. I. Hayakawa and Alan R. Hayakawa](https://www.amazon.com/Language-Thought-Action-S-I-Hayakawa/dp/0156482401/)

- [Trees, Maps, and Theorems: Effective Communication for Rational Minds, by Jean-Luc Doumont](https://www.principiae.be/book/)

### How to Act

- [Conscious Business, by Fred Kofman](https://www.amazon.com/Conscious-Business-Build-through-Values/dp/1622032020/)

- [A Guide to the Good Life, by William Irvine](https://www.amazon.com/Guide-Good-Life-Ancient-Stoic/dp/0195374614/)

- [Meditations, by Marcus Aurelius](https://www.amazon.com/Meditations-New-Translation-Marcus-Aurelius/dp/0812968255/)

### Arts and Design

- [Interaction of Color, by Josef Albers](https://www.amazon.com/Interaction-Color-Anniversary-Josef-Albers/dp/0300179359/)

### Food and Drink

- [The Food Lab, by J. Kenji Lopez-Alt](https://www.amazon.com/Food-Lab-Cooking-Through-Science/dp/0393081087/)

- [Death & Co: Modern Classic Cocktails, by David Kaplan and Nick Fauchald](https://www.amazon.com/Death-Co-Modern-Classic-Cocktails/dp/1607745259/)

- [Liquid Intelligence, by Dave Arnold](https://www.amazon.com/Liquid-Intelligence-Science-Perfect-Cocktail/dp/0393089037/)
